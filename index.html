    Momentum



Momentum, that drive the world keeps talking about. The energy, the force, the strength, the thrust that motivates us to burst the bubbles of our comfort zones and enforces us to thrive on our ambitions, to conquer our inhibitions and to achieve our ultimate goals. Momentum suggests motion, demands anything but stagnancy. And that's something we have been bred upon. From the sacred lessons in our Holy Vedas to the uptown contemporary writers these days, we are constantly hammered with the importance of momentum. Wherever you are, whatever you do, whichever place you dream of getting to, the answer is just one i.e. momentum. There is no surviving without it. It shapes us. There is no one who isn't engulfed by the embrace of progress. Be it in any field like social, economical, spiritual or educational. There is only one way with momentum, forward. And therefore we find ourselves at the epitome of curiosity as we intend to hear all about this from the masters of it who are our distinguished speakers.





    About TED



TED is a nonprofit devoted to spreading ideas, usually in the form of short, powerful talks (18 minutes or less). TED began in 1984 as a conference where Technology, Entertainment and Design converged, and today covers almost all topics — from science to business to global issues — in more than 100 languages. Meanwhile, independently run TEDx events help share ideas in communities around the world. TED is a nonpartisan nonprofit devoted to spreading ideas, usually in the form of short, powerful talks. TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TEDTalks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group.

Srushti Nerkar

Innovator & Child Prodigy

She is a social innovator and a child prodigy working on water conservation with some innovative tools invented by her. She had created a working prototype of a water-saving shower with save of upto 85% of the water in a regular shower bath.
